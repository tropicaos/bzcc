About Me

Hello! I am Laureano, an audiovisual producer from Paraíba, Brazil, and a student of Radio, TV, and Web at UFPB. I mainly focus on producing commercials, music videos, and documentaries. My original work has been presented at festivals such as CineCongo, Comunicurtas, and Mostra Sesc de Cinema, and my volunteer work has been aired on open TV, on Jornal da Band. Commercially, I have provided audiovisual services for the Administradores.com portal, the Marketing sector of Armazém Paraíba, and I was a post-producer at Colores Filmes, an audiovisual production company in João Pessoa. Besides end-to-end audiovisual production, I am also a communicator and offer services in strategic communication, operations management, and game design.
